Essence

As a "human-first" product designer, my core belief is to solve problems by applying design thinking and balancing the needs of the user with the needs of the business. I have a proven track record of successfully utilizing various design tools and methodologies such as user research, usability testing and visual design to create solutions that deliver both functional excellence and aesthetic appeal, all while meeting business objectives.

I am a proactive communicator with a strong ability to work in cross-functional teams. I bring a positive attitude, growth mindset, and a collaborative approach to my work. I seek ways to support my team and make sure our project is a success. ‍
